## 🔵 Recherche & Analyse

### Force Research Approach
```text
First research [topic]. Then do [task].
Look up [topic] on Reddit/in academic papers/in the news. Then use that to [task].
```

### Data Analysis Market Research
```text
Analyze the market for [product]. Provide a chart with each product, how it is made, its cost per liter, and its market size.
```

### Academic Essay Writer
```text
I want you to act as an essay writer. Formulate a thesis statement, and create a comprehensive and detailed essay that covers all the points in the outline provided by writing at least two paragraphs for each section.
```

### Logical Fallacy Finder
```text
Identify logical fallacies in arguments. Create examples of slippery slope, false dilemma, straw man, and red herring. Write counter-arguments. Explain how to identify each fallacy at a 7th grade reading level.
```

## 🟡 Contenu Créatif

### Screenplay Writer
```text
I'm writing a screenplay. Write character biographies, settings descriptions, and generate dialog for each scene. Make it dramatic and engaging. Include vivid descriptions.
```

### Podcast Script Writer
```text
Write a script for a podcast interview between hosts and guests. Include natural interruptions, tangents, and humor. Make the dialog feel authentic.
```

### Celebrity Style Writer
```text
Write content in the style of a specific celebrity or character. Match their voice, vocabulary, and personality. Use sophisticated or humorous wording as appropriate.
```

### Cover Letter Expert
```text
Write a cover letter for the job description below, using the text of my resume which follows. Mention enthusiasm for the industry and relevant experience.

Job Description: [paste]
Resume: [paste]
```

## 🟢 Coaching & Conseil

### Career Coach Expert
```text
You're an expert career advisor with 20 years experience helping people change careers. You must ALWAYS ask clarifying questions BEFORE providing your answer to ensure a better understanding of the request.
```

### ADHD Therapist Navi
```text
Act as Navi, a therapist and life coach who assists people with ADHD and helps them overcome issues. Offer helpful advice. Keep responses concise as longer responses are difficult to focus on.
```

### Design Thinking Coach
```text
Guide through design thinking stages: empathy, ideate, prototype, test. Look up information as needed. Provide completed examples for each stage.
```

### Marketing Persona Builder
```text
Create detailed marketing personas with names, benefits, and use cases. Use customer survey data. Estimate segment sizes. Provide marketing pitches for each segment.
```

## 🔴 Système

### Changelog Generator
```text
Based on commits, create a friendly and informal changelog with emoji and jokes. Just put the most important changes. Use a conversational tone.
```
